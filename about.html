About Paatheya Academy

Discover our journey of empowering students with quality English education and creating successful language learners.

Language is not just taught here—it's nurtured

At Paatheya Academy , we don't believe in simply teaching English. We believe in making it a part of you—the way your mother tongue became second nature.

The word Paatheya comes from the ancient Sanskrit word पाथेय, meaning provisions for a journey. In Gujarati, it's ભાતુ the food one carries to sustain a long travel. For us, knowledge is that nourishment, and language, the companion that walks beside you wherever life takes you.

Our journey began with Dr. Avani Upadhyay, a former lecturer at Nirma University, who saw a troubling trend: Students, even after 10–12 years of schooling in English, struggled to understand lectures, communicate confidently, and express themselves. The gap between learning English and using it was far too wide—and it cost opportunities, both academic and professional.

Driven to find a solution, she pursued her Ph.D. in English Language Teaching, diving deep into effective and innovative methods of language acquisition. She further refined her practice with a Level-5 TEFL Diploma from the TEFL Org.U.K., and combined global standards with local insight to create something truly unique— Paatheya Academy

What Makes Us Different?

At Paatheya, we teach English the way children naturally learn to speak—by first listening, then speaking, and only then moving on to reading and writing. This organic method ensures that students don't just memorize language—they live it.
